# SCDP User Guide

## Welcome to SCDP by Synozur

The Synozur Consulting Delivery Platform (SCDP) is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline the entire lifecycle of consulting projects. From initial estimation through final billing, SCDP helps you manage clients, track time, record expenses, and generate invoices efficiently.

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the SCDP login page
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click "Log In"

**Note**: Your account is created by system administrators. Contact IT Support at ITHelp@synozur.com if you need assistance with your login credentials.

### Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard showing:
- Active project assignments
- Recent time entries
- Pending expenses
- Important notifications and alerts

## My Workspace

Your personal workspace provides quick access to your daily tasks and activities.

### My Dashboard

**Purpose**: Overview of your current work and pending tasks

**Key Features**:
- Active assignments with deadlines
- Time tracking summary
- Pending expense submissions
- Quick access to frequently used features

### My Assignments

**Purpose**: View all projects and tasks assigned to you

**Key Features**:
- List of active project assignments
- Project details and timelines
- Resource allocation information
- Quick navigation to project details

### My Time

**Purpose**: Track time spent on projects and activities

**Recording Time**:
1. Navigate to "My Time"
2. Select the project and activity
3. Enter the date and hours worked
4. Add description of work performed
5. Click "Submit" to save your time entry

**Tips**:
- Enter time daily for accuracy
- Be descriptive in your work descriptions
- Review your time entries before period end
- Ensure all time is properly categorized

### My Expenses

**Purpose**: Submit and track expense reimbursements

**Submitting an Expense**:
1. Go to "My Expenses"
2. Click "New Expense"
3. Select the project (if applicable)
4. Choose expense category
5. Enter amount and date
6. Upload receipt image or PDF
7. Add description
8. Click "Submit"

**Expense Status**:
- **Pending**: Awaiting approval
- **Approved**: Approved for reimbursement
- **Rejected**: Requires correction or clarification
- **Paid**: Reimbursement processed

### My Projects

**Purpose**: View details of projects you're assigned to

**What You'll See**:
- Project name and client
- Your role on the project
- Project timeline and status
- Budget and resource allocation
- Project deliverables and milestones

## Portfolio Management (PM & Executive Roles)

### Portfolio Dashboard

**Purpose**: High-level overview of all organizational projects

**Key Metrics**:
- Active projects count
- Total revenue
- Resource utilization
- Project health indicators
- Financial performance

### All Projects

**Purpose**: Manage and track all consulting projects

**Creating a New Project**:
1. Navigate to "All Projects"
2. Click "New Project"
3. Select the client
4. Enter project name and description
5. Set start and end dates
6. Define project structure (epics, stages, workstreams)
7. Assign team members
8. Click "Create Project"

**Project Structure**:
SCDP uses customizable vocabulary for project organization:
- **Epics/Programs**: High-level project phases
- **Stages/Sprints**: Project execution stages
- **Workstreams/Tracks**: Parallel work areas
- **Milestones/Releases**: Key project checkpoints
- **Activities/Tasks**: Specific work items

*Note: Your organization may use custom terminology configured by administrators.*

### Clients

**Purpose**: Manage client relationships and information

**Adding a New Client**:
1. Go to "Clients"
2. Click "New Client"
3. Enter client name and contact information
4. Set billing contact details
5. Specify currency preference
6. Upload MSA (Master Services Agreement) if available
7. Upload NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) if applicable
8. Click "Save"

**Client Information Includes**:
- Contact details and addresses
- Billing configuration
- Active and historical projects
- Financial summary
- Legal documents (MSA, NDA)

### Estimates

**Purpose**: Create and manage project estimates for clients

**Creating an Estimate**:
1. Navigate to "Estimates"
2. Click "New Estimate"
3. Select client
4. Define project scope
5. Add resources and roles
6. Specify hours and rates
7. Include expenses and markup
8. Review total cost
9. Save as draft or finalize

**Estimate Status**:
- **Draft**: Work in progress
- **Final**: Ready for client review
- **Sent**: Delivered to client
- **Approved**: Client accepted
- **Rejected**: Client declined

### Resource Management

**Purpose**: Allocate and manage team members across projects

**Key Features**:
- View resource availability
- Assign resources to projects
- Track utilization rates
- Identify capacity constraints
- Plan future allocations

### Reports

**Purpose**: Access analytics and performance reports

**Available Reports**:
- Project performance
- Time utilization
- Expense summaries
- Resource allocation
- Financial analysis
- Client profitability

## Financial Operations (Billing Administrator Role)

### Billing & Invoicing

**Purpose**: Generate and manage client invoices

**Creating an Invoice**:
1. Navigate to "Billing & Invoicing"
2. Select the client
3. Choose billing period
4. Review time entries and expenses
5. Apply billing rates
6. Generate invoice
7. Export or email to client

**Invoice Management**:
- Track invoice status
- Record payments
- Manage billing batches
- Handle adjustments and credits

### Expense Management

**Purpose**: Review and approve employee expense submissions

**Approval Process**:
1. Review submitted expenses
2. Verify receipts and details
3. Approve or request clarification
4. Process for reimbursement
5. Allocate to appropriate projects

### Rate Management

**Purpose**: Configure billing and cost rates

**Rate Types**:
- **Rack Rates**: Standard billing rates by role
- **Custom Rates**: Client-specific or project-specific rates
- **Cost Rates**: Internal cost tracking

**Managing Rates**:
1. Navigate to "Rate Management"
2. Select role or resource
3. Set standard rate
4. Configure client overrides if needed
5. Save changes

## Administration (Administrator Role)

### User Management

**Purpose**: Manage user accounts and permissions

**Adding a User**:
1. Go to "User Management"
2. Click "Add User"
3. Enter user details (name, email, title)
4. Assign role
5. Set login permissions
6. Configure default rates
7. Click "Create User"

**User Properties**:
- **Can Login**: User can authenticate to the system
- **Is Assignable**: User can be assigned to projects
- **Is Active**: User account is active

### System Settings

**Purpose**: Configure platform-wide settings

**Settings Include**:
- Company name and branding
- Version information
- Default currency
- Organization vocabulary preferences
- System defaults

Configure terms for:

- Milestone (e.g., "Release", "Checkpoint", "Gate")
- Activity (e.g., "Task", "Story", "Work Item")

**Benefits**:
- Align with client terminology
- Match your organization's processes
- Improve communication clarity

### File Repository

**Purpose**: Centralized document management

**Features**:
- Upload and organize project documents
- SharePoint integration
- Version control
- Access permissions
- Document search

## Common Workflows

### Weekly Time Entry Workflow
1. Log in to SCDP
2. Navigate to "My Time"
3. Review projects you worked on
4. Enter time for each day
5. Add descriptions of work performed
6. Submit time entries
7. Verify submission confirmation

### Expense Reimbursement Workflow
1. Collect receipts for reimbursable expenses
2. Log in to "My Expenses"
3. Create new expense entry
4. Upload receipt
5. Assign to project (if applicable)
6. Submit for approval
7. Track approval status
8. Await reimbursement

### Project Setup Workflow (PM)
1. Create or verify client record
2. Create new project
3. Define project structure
4. Set up estimates if needed
5. Assign team members
6. Configure billing rates
7. Communicate project details to team
8. Begin tracking time and expenses

### Invoice Generation Workflow (Billing Admin)
1. Verify all time and expenses are submitted
2. Review time entries for accuracy
3. Approve pending expenses
4. Generate invoice for billing period
5. Review invoice details
6. Export or email to client
7. Track payment status
8. Record payment when received

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Contact your IT administrator at ITHelp@synozur.com to request a password reset.

**Q: Can I edit time entries after submission?**
A: This depends on your organization's policies. Contact your PM or administrator for guidance.

**Q: What file formats are accepted for receipts?**
A: Common image formats (JPG, PNG) and PDF files are supported.

**Q: How do I change my assigned role?**
A: Role assignments are managed by administrators. Request changes through your manager or IT Support.

**Q: Can I work on multiple projects simultaneously?**
A: Yes, you can be assigned to multiple projects and track time separately for each.

**Q: How often should I submit my time?**
A: Best practice is daily, but at minimum weekly to ensure accuracy.

**Q: What if I entered time on the wrong project?**
A: Contact your PM or billing administrator to correct the entry.

**Q: How do I download reports?**
A: Most reports include an export function. Look for download or export buttons within each report view.

**Q: Can clients access SCDP?**
A: No, SCDP is for internal use only. Invoices and reports are exported and shared with clients externally.

**Q: How do I request new features?**
A: Contact IT Support with your feature request or suggestion.
